Bottleneck Analysis · Terrance McMahon

Prepared 2026-05-12 by AG1 Consulting. Five leverage points across the terrancemcmahon.com brand, ranked by revenue impact.

The asymmetric finding: 1M+ cross-platform audience, 3x bestselling author, TEDx speaker, Re-Create mentoring app, former MassMutual Northern New England CEO. Four-plus products, no observable $-tier ladder sequencing them. This is not the "thin offer" pattern. This is the "multi-product / no-ladder" pattern. The structural lift is the explicit tier architecture across the catalogue.

Combined Year-1 floor: $280K-$900K across the five points.

Bottleneck 01 · No Visible $-Tier Ladder Despite Four-Plus Products

Classification: Install · Highest revenue lever in the package (asymmetric play).

What's happening

Cold traffic from the 1M+ cross-platform following lands on terrancemcmahon.com and faces a multi-product buffet: three books, the Re-Create mentoring app, the TEDx talk, the speaking and keynote business, and the implied private mentorship layer. The products exist. The ladder between them does not.

A reader who finishes one book has no observable path into the app. A viewer who watches the TEDx talk on YouTube has no observable path back into the books. A podcast listener has no observable path into mentorship. Each product is monetizing in isolation, which means the audience that wants to buy more cannot find the next tier without doing the routing themselves.

This is the structural opposite of the "thin offer" pattern. The IP is built. The audience is built. What is missing is the sequencing that walks audience between the products.

Cost of waiting

Conservative monthly leak: $8K-$25K in mid-tier and high-tier conversions not happening because the next-step is invisible to the audience.

Year-1 floor on the ladder install alone: $100K-$300K incremental, from the sequencing fix. Anchored on a single tier-routed nurture sequence (book reader to app, app graduate to private mentorship, private mentorship cohort feeding speaking inbound) plus the explicit ladder copy on the consolidated home.

6 months from now

Another six months of audience-yes / ladder-invisible inertia. Another six months of book readers walking past the app, app graduates walking past private mentorship, TEDx viewers walking past the books. Estimated loss across 6 months of inaction: $50K-$150K never collected in incremental conversion the ladder would have produced.

Fix

ONE clean four-tier ladder, anchored on the Algorithm-to-Recreate-Yourself methodology (the TEDx through-line):

  • Entry: Books (3x bestsellers, Amazon + free-chapter download with email capture)
  • Mid (recurring): Re-Create mentoring app
  • High-ticket signature: Private 1:1 / cohort mentorship with Terrance (application-only)
  • Enterprise: Speaking, keynotes, corporate retainer (B2B booking funnel)

Pricing stage-specific, confirmed at intake. Built in Terrance's bold + redemption-arc-anchored voice register, not in operator-bro register and not in pop-spiritual register (see copy-strategy for the AG1 universal banned-phrase list).

Bottleneck 02 · Book Audience Has No Observable Nurture Into The Re-Create App Or Mentorship

Classification: Install · Fast lift.

What's happening

Three bestselling books in market. The audience that buys a book is the warmest cohort he has access to, they've already invested time and money and they've read the methodology in long-form. There is no observable nurture sequence routing book readers into the Re-Create app or into mentorship.

Standard pattern across bestselling-author brands at this tier: free chapter download from the home page plus 14-day email sequence plus tier-routed CTA at the end. Without it, every book sold is a one-time transaction instead of the entry point into a recurring relationship.

The Amazon-buyer is the absolute warmest segment in the funnel after a TEDx viewer. The relationship currently ends at the back cover.

Cost of waiting

Conservative monthly leak: $3K-$10K in app subscriptions and mentorship inbound not happening from book-reader compound.

Year-1 floor on the book-to-app nurture install: $40K-$120K. Conservative math: 3 books in market, ongoing Amazon volume plus periodic promo spikes, even a small percent of those readers routing into the app at recurring cadence is a recoverable revenue layer.

6 months from now

Six more months of book readers exiting the relationship at the back cover. Estimated loss across 6 months of inaction: $20K-$60K never collected from a cohort already pre-qualified on the methodology.

Fix

Standard 14-day post-book-download nurture sequence:

  • Free first-chapter download from terrancemcmahon.com (email capture)
  • 8-touch nurture sequence over 14 days, methodology-anchored
  • Tier-routed CTAs at Day 5 (app), Day 10 (mentorship), Day 14 (full ladder view)
  • Companion landing pages per book for paid Amazon readers who scan the QR or follow the back-cover URL

Built once, runs forever. One of the lowest-friction installs in the package.

Bottleneck 03 · No Hero VSL Pre-Framing The Algorithm Methodology

Classification: Install · Compound pickup.

What's happening

Terrance holds one of the rarest credibility cocktails in the Christian-business-leadership category:

  • The literal redemption arc (350lb morbidly obese MassMutual CEO to liver transplant survivor to mentor)
  • The TEDx-anchored Algorithm methodology ("From Broken to Beautiful; An Algorithm to Recreate Yourself")
  • 3x bestselling author (the IP is in market in long-form)
  • Faith-explicit, Bible-led positioning (the differentiator in the personal-development category)

All of this currently lives on the TEDx YouTube page, in the books, and in IG captions. There is no hero video walking a cold prospect through the methodology in 4-7 minutes on terrancemcmahon.com. The TEDx talk exists but it lives on YouTube outside his owned funnel, which means the strongest credibility asset he has is doing zero work on the cold-traffic home page.

A hero VSL on the consolidated home, pre-framing the algorithm methodology and the literal redemption arc in the founder's own voice, is the highest-leverage proof asset he can install. It is also the cheapest (single recording session, lives forever).

Cost of waiting

Conservative monthly leak: $3K-$10K in cold-traffic conversion drift. Text-and-image-only home pages typically convert cold traffic at 1-2% on email opt-in for this category. With a hero VSL pre-framing the methodology plus the literal redemption arc, the floor moves to 3-5%.

Year-1 floor on the hero VSL install: $40K-$120K.

6 months from now

Estimated loss across 6 months of inaction: $20K-$60K never collected from the same traffic. The redemption-arc origin story is the Hot Girl proof asset for this brand, and it is currently doing 10% of the work it could do.

Fix

Hero VSL (4-7 min) on the consolidated home. Beats: the literal redemption arc (90 days to live plus liver transplant plus 350lb to healthy plus MassMutual seat) plus the Algorithm-to-Recreate-Yourself framework plus the four-tier ladder plus the implicit CTA. Plus shorter 90-second cutdowns for the app landing, the mentorship landing, the speaking landing.

Recorded once per surface, lives forever.

Bottleneck 04 · X / LinkedIn Long-Form Ghostwriting From The TEDx + Book + Speaking Catalogue

Classification: Recurring Engagement · Compounds monthly.

What's happening

Terrance has rich IP in market: 3 books, the TEDx talk, the Re-Create app methodology, speaking decks, podcast appearances. The repurpose pipeline from this catalogue into X long-form thread cadence and LinkedIn essay cadence is the recurring distribution layer that compounds the funnel past install.

Two specific lanes:

(a) LinkedIn corporate-keynote lane. Former MassMutual Northern New England CEO plus TEDx speaker plus 3x bestselling author is one of the strongest LinkedIn-credential cocktails in the Christian-business-leadership category. Corporate keynote contracts run $10K-$50K each. The LinkedIn cadence at the executive register is the inbound funnel for those bookings. Current cadence not directly verified, flag for confirmation, but the lane is either underweight or absent.

(b) X long-form thread lane. The Christian-business and faith-business cohort lives on X. Long-form threads from the book catalogue plus the TEDx algorithm framework plus the redemption arc are the natural format. Current cadence not directly verified, flag for confirmation.

Repurpose pipeline math: one weekly long-form recording session feeds 2-3 IG Reels plus 1 LinkedIn essay plus 1 X long-form thread plus 3-5 Threads posts plus podcast clips. One session, six surfaces fed.

Cost of waiting

Conservative monthly leak: $7K-$25K, mostly from B2B corporate-keynote contracts walking past on the LinkedIn feed.

Year-1 floor on the recurring distribution layer: $80K-$300K. A single corporate keynote engagement is worth $10K-$50K. Two to six engagements per year sourced from a LinkedIn cadence at the executive register plus the X long-form audience compounding into Re-Create app subscribers is the conservative floor.

6 months from now

Another six months of competing Christian-business-leadership voices out-publishing him on LinkedIn and X. The gap compounds. Estimated loss across 6 months of inaction: $40K-$150K in distribution-driven revenue never collected.

Fix

Monthly ongoing engagement covering:

  • LinkedIn cadence 3-4x per week at the executive register (positioning for corporate-keynote inbound)
  • X long-form thread cadence 1-2x per week (faith-business audience capture)
  • Repurpose pipeline from books, TEDx, podcast appearances, app methodology to LinkedIn, X, IG Reels, Threads
  • One weekly long-form recording session feeds the whole pipeline

Bundled with the Install side of the engagement.

Bottleneck 05 · Speaker / TEDx Capture Funnel

Classification: Install · Lowest-friction lever.

What's happening

The TEDx talk ("From Broken to Beautiful; An Algorithm to Recreate Yourself") is the single highest-warmth piece of long-form content in the brand. A viewer who finishes the TEDx talk is the warmest cohort he has access to, they've already invested 15-20 minutes with the founder's voice and they've heard the methodology end-to-end.

The TEDx talk lives on YouTube outside his owned funnel. There is no observable post-talk landing page, no opt-in, no nurture sequence routing TEDx viewers into the books, the app, or the mentorship. The same pattern applies to keynote audiences (people who attend a live talk) and podcast listeners.

Three of the highest-warmth audience surfaces in his stack are exiting the relationship the moment the talk, episode, or keynote ends.

Cost of waiting

Conservative monthly leak: $1.5K-$5K from warmest-segment listeners and viewers walking past with no email capture, no episode-mapped sequence, no routing into the offer ladder.

Year-1 floor on the speaker / TEDx capture install: $20K-$60K. Modest in absolute terms because the channel-by-channel volume is small, but cost-per-acquisition is near zero (the listener is already pre-qualified), so the margin compounds across every future TEDx watch, podcast appearance, and live keynote.

6 months from now

Estimated loss across 6 months of inaction: $10K-$30K in warm-audience inventory walked past. Plus the lost network effect of every podcast host and event producer whose audience would have routed cleanly into the funnel had the capture mechanism existed.

Fix

Standard 14-day post-talk / post-episode nurture sequence:

  • Companion landing page per major asset (TEDx talk, each podcast appearance, each live keynote)
  • Lightweight opt-in capture, asset-mapped
  • 14-day email nurture routing into the books (entry) or the Re-Create app (mid)
  • Co-promo template for each podcast host, event producer, or speaker bureau (they get a clean opt-in URL to share)

Built once, reused across every future appearance.

Notes On Sequencing

The largest lever (Bottleneck 01, the explicit ladder) is the structural rearrangement, not a build-from-scratch. The products already exist. What ships in Phase One and Two is the routing between them.

Bottlenecks 02, 03, and 05 ship inside the first 10 days and start producing first revenue inside the first 14-30 days. Bottleneck 04 is the recurring layer that compounds past install and is where the corporate-keynote inbound lands in the Day 60-90 window (LinkedIn-led B2B cycles are 30-90 days, not 30 days).

This is why Phase One front-loads the hero VSL plus consolidated home plus free chapter capture (Bottlenecks 02 plus 03), Phase Two installs the explicit four-tier ladder and the post-talk capture (Bottlenecks 01 plus 05), and Phase Four is where the largest single recurring result (the first corporate-keynote engagement plus the first private mentorship cohort) lands.

End of bottleneck analysis.
